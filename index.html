Exploring Top Fifty Songs in 2019

YuQi Huang

The dataset contains the most popular songs TOP 50 in 2019, which can be downloaded on the Kaggle website (https:https://www.kaggle.com/leonardopena/top50spotify2019). The objective of this project is to illustrate a story about the top songs in different categories. It aims to help users find their favorite music in 2019.
